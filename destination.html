Travelogue: Paris

Overview

Paris is the capital of France and one of the most popular travel destinations in the world. The city is known for its art, food, and historic landmarks.

Must-See Spots

  1. Eiffel Tower
  2. Louvre Museum
  3. Notre-Dame Cathedral
  4. Montmartre
  5. Champs-Élysées

The Eiffel Tower overlooking Paris.

Visitor Tips

  • Learn a few basic French phrases.
  • Use public transportation.
  • Buy tickets in advance.
  • Wear comfortable shoes.
Paris was unforgettable. Every street felt like a dream.
